West Highland White Terriers, commonly known as Westies, are small and spirited terrier dogs renowned for their charming appearance and vivacious personalities. These adorable white-coated canines originated in Scotland, where they were bred for their strong hunting instincts and tenacious character. Westies are characterized by their distinctive double-layered white fur, erect triangular ears, and bright, expressive eyes. They are compact yet muscular, with a friendly disposition and a penchant for playfulness. Westies are known for their independence, intelligence, and feisty nature, making them excellent companions for active families. They have a strong prey drive, and their keen sense of smell and natural curiosity can sometimes lead to digging or exploring. While they may be headstrong, they are also loyal, affectionate, and make wonderful pets for those willing to engage in their spirited activities.
